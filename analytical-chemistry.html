Analytical Chemistry

Analytical chemistry is the branch of chemistry that involves the analysis of substances to determine their composition and quantify the amount of each component.

  • Qualitative Analysis: Identifying the components of a sample.
  • Quantitative Analysis: Measuring the quantities of the components in a sample.
  • Chromatography: A technique used to separate the components of a mixture.
