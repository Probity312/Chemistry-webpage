Introduction to Chemistry

Chemistry is the scientific discipline involved with elements and compounds composed of atoms, molecules, and ions: their composition, structure, properties, behavior, and the changes they undergo during a reaction with other substances.

What is Chemistry?

Chemistry is a branch of science that studies the properties, composition, and transformation of matter. It is a central science that bridges other natural sciences, including physics, geology, and biology.

Importance of Chemistry

Chemistry is crucial because it helps us understand the world around us. It is essential for the development of new materials, medicines, energy solutions, and many other applications that improve our quality of life.
