Famous Creators of Chemistry

1. Antoine Lavoisier

Born: August 26, 1743
Died: May 8, 1794

Antoine Lavoisier is considered the father of modern chemistry. He discovered the role of oxygen in combustion and helped develop the metric system.

2. Marie Curie

Born: November 7, 1867
Died: July 4, 1934

Marie Curie is best known for her work on radioactivity, and she was the first woman to win a Nobel Prize.

3. Dmitri Mendeleev

Born: February 8, 1834
Died: February 2, 1907

Dmitri Mendeleev is the creator of the Periodic Table of Elements. His table allowed for the prediction of elements yet to be discovered.

4. Robert Boyle

Born: January 25, 1627
Died: December 31, 1691

Robert Boyle is known for Boyle’s Law, which describes the relationship between pressure and volume in gases.
