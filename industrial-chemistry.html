Industrial Chemistry

Industrial chemistry applies chemical and physical processes to transform raw materials into valuable products. It is a key aspect of manufacturing and production industries.

  • Catalysis: The acceleration of chemical reactions by catalysts in industrial processes.
  • Petrochemicals: Chemicals derived from petroleum and natural gas, used in a wide range of products.
  • Pharmaceuticals: The development and production of drugs and medications.
