Chemistry in Daily Life

Chemistry is an integral part of our daily lives, influencing everything from the food we eat to the products we use.

  • Food Chemistry: The study of the chemical processes and interactions of all biological and non-biological components of foods.
  • Cosmetics: The chemical formulation of beauty and personal care products.
  • Cleaning Products: The chemicals used in detergents, soaps, and other cleaning agents.
