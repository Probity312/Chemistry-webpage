Quantum Chemistry

Quantum chemistry is the application of quantum mechanics to chemical systems, providing a theoretical framework for understanding molecular behavior.

  • Wavefunctions: Mathematical functions that describe the quantum state of a system.
  • Computational Methods: Techniques such as density functional theory (DFT) and molecular dynamics simulations used to solve quantum chemical equations.
  • Applications: Quantum chemistry contributes to drug discovery, materials science, and the development of new technologies.
