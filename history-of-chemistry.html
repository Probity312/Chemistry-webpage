History of Chemistry

The history of chemistry spans thousands of years and encompasses the discovery of elements, the development of the periodic table, and the advancement of chemical theories.

  • Ancient Chemistry: Early practices such as alchemy and metallurgy.
  • The Scientific Revolution: The establishment of modern chemistry in the 17th and 18th centuries.
  • Modern Chemistry: The development of chemical theories and technologies in the 19th and 20th centuries.
