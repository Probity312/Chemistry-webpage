Nanotechnology

Nanotechnology involves the manipulation of matter at the atomic and molecular scale to create materials, devices, and systems with unique properties and functions.

  • Nanomaterials: Substances with dimensions on the nanoscale, exhibiting novel physical, chemical, and biological properties.
  • Applications: Used in medicine (drug delivery systems), electronics (nanoelectronics), and environmental protection (nanosensors).
  • Ethical and Safety Considerations: Addressing concerns related to the environmental impact and potential health risks of nanotechnology.
