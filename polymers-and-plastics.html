Polymers and Plastics

Polymers are large molecules composed of repeating structural units, while plastics are synthetic polymers that can be molded into various shapes.

  • Natural Polymers: Polymers found in nature, such as proteins, cellulose, and natural rubber.
  • Synthetic Polymers: Man-made polymers created through chemical reactions, including polyethylene, PVC, and nylon.
  • Applications: Plastics are used in packaging, construction, electronics, and numerous other industries due to their versatility and durability.
