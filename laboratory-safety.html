Laboratory Safety

Laboratory safety is critical for preventing accidents and ensuring a safe working environment for scientists and researchers.

  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE): Gear such as lab coats, gloves, and goggles to protect against chemical exposure.
  • Safety Protocols: Procedures for handling hazardous materials and dealing with emergencies.
  • Waste Disposal: Proper methods for disposing of chemical waste to prevent contamination and harm.
